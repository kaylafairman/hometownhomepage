Top three activities to do in Greece

The Navagio Beach

The Navagio Beach in Zakynthosn, Greece is a great place to swim, scuba dive, go on boat-rides.

The Melissani Cave

The Melissani Cave in Greece is a great place to step out of your comfort zone and sight-see, swim, and experience going on tiny wooden boats.

The Parthenon

The Parthenon, in Acropolis, Greece is considered to be the most important surviving building of Classical Greece. Therefore, it would be a great place to visit, as it is a once on a lifetime experience.
